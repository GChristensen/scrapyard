v0.10 April 21, 2021

  • Restored the DOM Eraser tool at the archived page edit toolbar.
  • Added an option to not display the edit toolbar by default. The toolbar can always be toggled with the Ctrl+Alt+T key combination.
  • The size in bytes is shown for the newly archived pages at the "More" section of the property dialog. The number should be considered as a rough approximation of the item size saved in UTF-8 format. Due to the specifics of IndexedDB, the real disk size of the stored content is usually 1.5 times greater and could not be known exactly. Size of the formerly saved items could be calculated at the advanced settings. Please, do not forget to backup.
